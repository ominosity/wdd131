Wild Western Oregon Siteplan

Site Name: Wild Western Oregon (wildwesternoregon.com)

I chose this name because the site will focus on outdoor and culinary adventures available in the western half of oregon, from the western slopes of the Cascades to the Coast.

Site Purpose

This site provides pictures and descriptions of the places that my wife and I have travelled in Western Oregon, with the purpose of providing travel recommendations to potential visitors. Focus will be on three core categories:

  1. The Coast
  2. Waterfalls
  3. Culinary Destinations

It will also have a newsletter sign-up form to hear about new adventures as they occur.

Scenarios

  • Where can I get the best clam chowder on the Oregon coast?
  • Where can I see great waterfalls in or near the Willamette valley?

Color Schema

  • #1f3039 - The primary color of the site. This will be the color of the text when on the light background and the background color of the header and footer.
  • #e5e7ec - The primary background color of the site. This will also be used as the text color for headers and other elements on dark backgrounds.
  • #214156 - The secondary color of the site. This will be used as the background color for headings H2 - H6.

Typography

  • Headers and form labels will use a semi-bold "Playfair Display" font.
  • Body elements and forms will use the "Alice" font.
