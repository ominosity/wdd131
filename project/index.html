About Us

We are Richard and Lyndsie Burgener, and this website is our chance to share the natural and culinary wonders we've discovered in the western half of the state of Oregon.

From our home in Albany, we've explored much of the the Willamette Valley along I-5 and up and down the coast on the Pacific Coast Highway. It seems like every trip brings beautiful sights, life, and majesty, and we are excited to share our wonder with you.

Why Oregon?

Several years ago, we traveled from our lifetime state of Utah to explore the Oregon coast. Every day we were treated to a new amazing sight, from the rugged coastline to the lush old-growth vegetation to waterfalls.

At the end of our trip, we had planned to spend a couple of nights in California, our favorite vacation state for our entire marriage. This trip was different. As soon as we crossed the state line, we felt like we had left something special behind.

As we talked on the very long drive back to Utah, we realized that we had both had the same feeling at some part of our trip. That somehow, however unlikely, Oregon felt like it should be "home". Within a couple of months, we were building a house in the city of Albany and moved in 5 months later.
